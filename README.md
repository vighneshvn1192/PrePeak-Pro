# User Guide

## Features Overview

### 🎯 Prediction Model

**What it does:**
Analyzes any product/keyword for market viability

**How to use:**
1. Click "PREDICTION MODEL" tab
2. Enter keyword (e.g., "wireless earbuds")
3. Optional: Select category
4. Click "RUN PREDICTION ANALYSIS"
5. Wait 10 seconds
6. View comprehensive report

**Output:**
- 🟢/🔴 GREEN/RED light status
- Search volume (monthly)
- Saturation score (0-100%)
- Opportunity score (0-100%)
- Top 3-5 competitors
- 12-month trend chart
- Market analysis report
- Actionable recommendation

### 📊 Saturation Index

**What it does:**
Extracts competitor intelligence from any URL

**How to use:**
1. Click "SATURATION INDEX" tab
2. Enter competitor URL
3. Enter your product (optional)
4. Click "ANALYZE COMPETITOR"
5. Wait 10 seconds
6. View intelligence report

**Output:**
- Competitor name & category
- Pricing analysis
- Market position
- Strengths & weaknesses
- Your differentiation strategy
- Market opportunities
